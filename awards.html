Division Program Quality Awards: Recognizing Excellence in Leadership and Achievement

Division Program Quality Awards for Members


  • Speechcraft Warrior: Members who have successfully conducted Speechcraft programs, empowering others to develop their public speaking skills.
  • YLP Warrior: Members who have led Youth Leadership Programs, inspiring and motivating young minds.
  • Triple Crown Awards: Members who have completed 3 pathways, demonstrating exceptional commitment and dedication.
  • Distinguished Toastmaster: Members who have achieved the highest recognition award, showcasing their outstanding leadership and communication skills.

Division Program Quality Awards for Clubs


  • Pathways Expert: Clubs with 100% Pathway Adoption, demonstrating a strong commitment to member growth and development.
  • Fast 5: Clubs that achieve 5 DCP Points by 31st December, showcasing their speed and agility in achieving club goals.
  • Super Club: Clubs that achieve Distinguished Status with April dues paid by 15th January, demonstrating exceptional leadership and management.

Division Program Quality Awards for Areas


  • Pathways Champion: Areas with 100% Pathways Adoption, leading the way in member growth and development.
  • Awesome Area: Areas that achieve President's Distinguished Area Award by 15th January, demonstrating outstanding leadership and achievement.
