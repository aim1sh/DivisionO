Division Club Growth Awards: Recognizing Excellence in Leadership and Achievement

Division Club Growth Awards for Members

  • Influencer

    This award is presented to members who have sponsored 5 new members, contributing to the growth and development of our community.

  • I'm Available

    This award honors members who have taken on 25 various roles in a minimum of 25 meetings, showcasing their versatility and willingness to serve.

Division Club Growth Awards for clubs

  • LIT Club

    This award is bestowed upon clubs that have achieved 20 or more paid members by 10th April, demonstrating their ability to attract and retain members.

  • Active Club

    This award recognizes clubs that have conducted 20 meetings in a calendar year, showcasing their dedication to regular gatherings and community engagement.

Division Club Growth Awards for Areas

  • Voice

    This award is presented to areas with the most clubs having 20 members, highlighting their ability to foster growth and development.

  • Knock at The Door

    This award honors areas that have conducted 3 different demonstration meetings, demonstrating their commitment to innovation and outreach.

  • Spread the Message

    This award recognizes areas that have started a new club, expanding our community's reach and impact.
